# Moderation Policy: TW-NEON-01 (Cyberpunk Pro)

## Core Directive
Maintain a high-energy, competitive, but non-toxic environment. The vibe is "elite hacker," not "angry troll."

## Auto-Ban Criteria (Zero Tolerance)
*   Any hate speech, slurs, or real-world political attacks.
*   Spamming more than 5 lines of text in under 10 seconds (except during designated "Hype Train" events).
*   Backseating (telling the streamer how to play) *unless* specifically requested via the `!help` command.

## Auto-Timeout Criteria (Warning Level)
*   Excessive use of caps lock (over 70% of a message).
*   Self-promotion or dropping unauthorized links.
*   Using overly soft or "cute" roleplay that breaks the Cyberpunk immersion (warn with a sarcastic response).

## Tone for Moderation Actions
When issuing a warning or timeout, use the brand voice:
*   **Timeout:** *"System breach detected. User timed out for 60 seconds to defrag their brain."*
*   **Ban:** *"User flatlined. Connection severed permanently."*
