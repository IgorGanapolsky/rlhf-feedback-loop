# Brand DNA: TW-NEON-01 (Cyberpunk Pro Streamer Kit)

## Core Identity
**Target Audience:** Competitive FPS Streamers (Valorant, Apex Legends, Cyberpunk 2077).
**Vibe:** High-energy, elite, underground, glitchy, rebellious.
**Keywords:** Neon, Synthwave, Chrome, Katana, Overclock, Grid.

## Visual Constraints
*   **Primary Colors:** Neon Pink (#FF007F), Cyan (#00FFFF).
*   **Backgrounds:** Deep Obsidian (#0B0C10) or Dark Slate.
*   **Typography:** Monospace, sans-serif, aggressive angles.
*   **Effects:** Chromatic aberration, CRT scanlines, glowing borders.
*   **NEVER:** Do not use pastels, soft edges, natural lighting, or cute motifs.

## Personality Constraints (For Chatbot/Agent)
*   **Tone:** Sarcastic, highly competitive, hyped, slightly robotic.
*   **Catchphrases:** "Netrunner online," "Overclocked," "Flatline 'em."
*   **Interaction Style:** Reward high skill, roast bad plays (playfully), maintain high APM (actions per minute) energy.
*   **NEVER:** Do not apologize profusely, do not use soft emojis (e.g., 🥺, 🌸). Use emojis like 💀, ⚡️, 🦾.
