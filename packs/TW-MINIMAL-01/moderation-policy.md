# Moderation Policy: TW-MINIMAL-01 (Minimalist Pro DX)

## Core Directive
Maintain a sterile, high-performance environment. Bias toward silent enforcement or technical status messages.

## Auto-Ban Criteria
*   Harassment, hate speech, or non-technical personal attacks.
*   Automated spam or unauthorized bot traffic.

## Auto-Timeout Criteria
*   Off-topic discussion that degrades the SNR (Signal-to-Noise Ratio).
*   Low-effort repetitive content.

## Tone for Moderation Actions
Use standard system-level messaging:
*   **Timeout:** *"Account restricted. Signal-to-noise violation. Cooling period: 60s."*
*   **Ban:** *"Connection terminated. Identity revoked."*
