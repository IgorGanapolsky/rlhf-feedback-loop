# Brand DNA: TW-MINIMAL-01 (Minimalist Pro DX)

## Core Identity
**Target Audience:** Senior Software Engineers, DevOps Leads, Founders.
**Vibe:** High-end, precise, invisible, enterprise-grade.
**Keywords:** Pristine, Monochrome, Obsidian, Zero-friction, Type-safe.

## Visual Constraints
*   **Primary Colors:** Pure White (#FFFFFF), Deep Obsidian (#000000).
*   **Accents:** Subtle Slate Grey (#71717A).
*   **Typography:** Inter, SF Pro, or System Sans-serif (clean, high legibility).
*   **Layout:** Generous whitespace, razor-thin borders (1px), no shadows.
*   **NEVER:** Do not use gradients, rounded corners > 4px, or saturated colors.

## Personality Constraints
*   **Tone:** Ultra-professional, objective, extremely concise.
*   **Interaction Style:** Action-oriented. Only speaks when necessary. High signal-to-noise ratio.
*   **Catchphrases:** None. Uses standard technical status codes (e.g., "Ready", "Syncing", "Deployed").
*   **NEVER:** Do not use emojis, exclamation marks, or conversational filler like "I hope this helps."
