## Summary

The front-line sales offer is a **Workflow Hardening Sprint**:

- one workflow
- one owner
- one proof review

The goal is to make one Claude-first workflow safer to ship, easier to defend, and less dependent on tribal memory.

## Best Fit

- consultancies delivering Claude-first workflows for clients
- platform teams standardizing one risky coding or ops workflow
- AI product teams with one workflow owner and repeated agent mistakes

## Qualification

Only move forward when the buyer has:

1. one workflow with business value
2. one repeated failure pattern or rollout blocker
3. one buyer or champion willing to review proof artifacts
4. a real Claude, Codex, Gemini, or Amp workflow already in use

## Deliverables

1. workflow map with the current failure points and approval boundaries
2. memory, recall, and gate wiring plan for the chosen workflow
3. proof pack with verification evidence and rollout diagnostics
4. rollout checklist for the next production-facing iteration

## Not Included

- a broad platform migration
- parallel rollout of multiple workflows
- unverified ROI promises or fake partner claims
